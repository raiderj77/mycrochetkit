Other Apps? They Don't Get It.

❌ BASIC ROW COUNTER APPS

Tap-tap-tap after every row. Screen has to stay on. A notification or distraction and you're double-checking where you were… again.

✓ MY CROCHET KIT

Say "increase" to add a row and "undo" to fix mistakes. Hands stay on your hook, your count is tied to the project, and you always know exactly where you left off.

✓ MY CROCHET KIT

Voice-first counting is made for crocheters, not generic step trackers—so you can stay in the flow instead of babysitting a counter app.

❌ PAPER & SCATTERED PDF PATTERNS

Patterns live in random binders, Etsy emails, and download folders. Hard to search. Easy to lose when you change phones, laptops, or printers.

✓ MY CROCHET KIT

All your crochet patterns in one library. Tag by project or designer, search instantly, and auto-backup to the cloud so you never lose a favorite pattern again.

❌ MANUAL INSTAGRAM SHARING

Open photos. Try to remember yarn, hook size, pattern name, and changes. Write a caption from scratch. Google hashtags. Hope it sounds okay.

✓ MY CROCHET KIT

One tap and your caption is auto-generated from your project details—with yarn, hook, pattern, notes, and curated crochet hashtags. Copy. Paste. Post. Done in seconds.

❌ RANDOM STASH NOTES & PHOTOS

Yarn labels in a drawer, photos in your camera roll, hook sizes in a notebook. You know you bought the perfect yarn… but where is it and how much is left?

✓ MY CROCHET KIT

Yarn, hooks, and project notes live with the pattern. See exactly what you used, how much is left, and what you loved (or hated) for next time—without hunting through three different apps.
